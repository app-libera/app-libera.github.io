Privacy Policy & GDPR

This web site processes your personal data under the Contract Lawful Basis of GDPR, as follows:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to anonymously track the number of web site visitors, and the number of visitors to a specific page.

To ensure that this data is anonymous and compliant with GDPR, we:

Do not log IP addresses,

Do not track web site visitors to a specific User ID

Enquiries

When submitting an inquiry, we request, store and process the following personal information:

"Firstname" "Lastname" "Email" "Subject" "Message"

We retain this information indefinitely, to build customer history and provide improved support (for example, if a new support request relates to an old one, we are then able to review the historical information to provide quicker, more accurate support).

What about "cookies"?

Cookies are small pieces of information that your browser stores on your computer's hard drive. Our cookies do not contain any personally identifiable information. Most web browsers accept cookies automatically, but you can usually change your browser to prevent this.

LiberaChat disclose the information you collect to third parties?

We will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission or are required by law to do so.

We may use your personal information to send you promotional information about third parties which we think you may find interesting if you tell us that you wish this to happen.

In Summary:

LiberaChat is committed to protecting your privacy. We use the information we collect on the site to make your overall experience possible. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your Consent:

By using our website, you consent to the collection and use of this information by LiberaChat. If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes on this page so that you are always aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances we disclose it.
